Life in Pune as a Firmware engineer, on a normal weekday.

1. 6.30 am , wake up, drink black coffee made by myself. 

2. Leave for office by 8.30 am without a tiffin because I am 22 (contrary to a popular misconception).

3. Reach office by 8:45. Start working on small things by 9:15.

4. 10:30 am scrum call. Resume work by 11:00 AM. 

5. At 1.00 pm, eat lunch with the office gang. 

6. 2-5:00 pm, continue with tasks, wit a coffee break at 4:00 PM.

7. 5 pm, start wrapping up things for the day, and prepare to leave.

8. 6.00 pm, leave for the Gym, reach there by 6:30 pm.

9. Workout till 8:00 PM. GO back home and make dinner by 9:00 PM.

10. If there's still things left to learn or study, make another shot of coffee after dinner and get to work.
11. Sleep by 1:00 AM at the latest.
