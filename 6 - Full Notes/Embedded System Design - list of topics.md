# Embedded Systems Design Series 🚀

## 📌 Overview
This series is aimed at improving system-level thinking in embedded engineering.  
Each week, we take a real-world product or subsystem and break down its design, trade-offs, and architecture.

---

## 🔁 System Roadmap (10 Weeks)

#### 1. Smart LED Bulb (WiFi/BLE)

#### 3. Game Controller (Wired + Wireless)

## 🧠 Suggested Structure for Each Article

1. Problem Definition  
2. High-Level Architecture (Block Diagram)  
3. Component Selection (MCU, sensors, ICs)  
4. Data Flow & Communication  
5. Power Design  
6. Failure Cases & Safety  
7. Trade-offs (Cost vs Performance vs Power)  
8. "If I Had to Build It" (Your Design Choices)
