It's officially been a week. I have had enough time to reflect on the move, and settle myself at the office. I write this to document what's going on and how an I adapting to things. And in the hopes that my audience who reads along these shall take away a thing or two from this.

And I haven't even touched the software part of things. I have written git-hooks and have contributed to the internal software policy, have helped in designing the software architecture for the upcoming projects, and shall get to writing and shipping code soon enough. 

On the "apart-from-work" side of things, the conversations and the train-of-thought that people around me have is something that I have never experienced before. There's being laser-focused on the task at hand; and there's these guys. IITians after-all, what else did I expect? It's an environment of calculated execution, moves being made at a rapid but a well thought out fashion, and advancements being made daily, on the personal and professional side of things. 

Let's see how this ride turns out to be when it's all said and done, shall we?
